Public Design-Learning Platform / Digital Resource Practice

Independent 3D & AI Resource Platform

I built a public-facing 3D and AI-assisted design resource platform across Xiaohongshu, Bilibili and a personal resource website. The platform translates Blender, GIS, AI-assisted workflows and visual prototyping processes into public tutorials, design-process content and free downloadable project files.

Challenge

Making technical workflows easier to learn, reproduce and adapt.

Many learners struggle to understand 3D, Blender, GIS and AI-assisted workflows because tutorials often show final results without reusable files or workflow context. The challenge was to make complex technical workflows easier to learn, reproduce and adapt through public tutorials and downloadable project resources.

My Role

Independent creator, producer and platform operator.

I independently planned topics, tested workflows, produced tutorial videos, packaged project files, published resources, responded to user questions and managed collaboration enquiries from brands, websites, AI tools and creator platforms.
