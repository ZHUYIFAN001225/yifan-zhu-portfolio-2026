Challenge

Making technical workflows easier to learn, reproduce and adapt.

Many learners struggle to understand 3D, Blender, GIS and AI-assisted workflows because tutorials often show final results without reusable files or workflow context. The challenge was to make complex technical workflows easier to learn, reproduce and adapt through public tutorials and downloadable project resources.

Process

From workflow testing to public resource distribution.

  1. Topic selection based on learner interest and workflow value
  2. Blender / GIS / AI workflow testing and repeatable process design
  3. Tutorial recording and design-process communication
  4. Project file packaging for reuse
  5. Publishing on Bilibili and Xiaohongshu / RED
  6. Free resource distribution through a personal resource website
  7. User questions, feedback and collaboration enquiries informing future content
